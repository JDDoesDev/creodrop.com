# Plans and Tiers

Creodrop offers four plans to match different needs and budgets. All plans include a 14-day free trial with no credit card required.

## Plan Comparison

| Feature | Starter | Standard | Pro | Developer |
|---------|---------|----------|-----|-----------|
| CPU | 0.5 cores | 1 core | 2 cores | 2 cores |
| Memory | 256 MB | 512 MB | 1 GB | 2 GB |
| PHP Workers | 2 | 4 | 8 | 8 |
| Max Upload | 32 MB | 64 MB | 128 MB | 128 MB |
| Environments | Production | Production | Production | Dev + Stage + Prod |
| Custom Domain | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Backups | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| SSL | Automatic | Automatic | Automatic | Automatic |

## Choosing a Plan

**Starter** -- You need a simple website. A personal blog, a portfolio, or a small brochure site with modest traffic (around 100 visits per day).

**Standard** -- You run a small business or organization. A restaurant site, a local shop, or a site with light e-commerce (100 to 1,000 visits per day). This is the plan most users start with.

**Pro** -- You have a busy site with significant traffic. Active e-commerce, a membership site, or a content-heavy site (1,000 to 10,000 visits per day).

**Developer** -- You are a developer or agency that needs full control. SSH access, git-based deployments, Drush CLI, and separate development/staging/production environments. See [Developer Tier](/docs/developer-tier) for details.

## What Every Plan Includes

- Managed Drupal hosting with automatic updates
- Automatic SSL certificates via Let's Encrypt
- Custom domain support with DNS verification
- Manual and scheduled database backups
- Real-time container health monitoring
- Control panel access for site management

## Upgrading or Downgrading

To change your plan, contact support. Your site will be migrated to the new resource allocation with minimal downtime.
