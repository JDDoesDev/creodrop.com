# Creating Your Account

## Sign Up

Visit the Creodrop registration page and create your account using one of these methods:

- **Google** -- Click "Continue with Google" to sign up with your Google account
- **GitHub** -- Click "Continue with GitHub" to sign up with your GitHub account
- **Email and password** -- Fill in the registration form with your name, email, and a password (minimum 8 characters)

All new accounts start with a **14-day free trial**. No credit card is required.

## Email Verification

After registering with email and password, you will be redirected to a verification page. Check your inbox for a verification email and click the link inside.

If you do not see the email:

- Check your spam or junk folder
- Make sure you entered the correct email address
- Wait a few minutes and click "Resend verification email" (available once per minute)

The verification link expires after 24 hours. If it expires, you can request a new one from the verification page.

## Logging In

After verifying your email, log in at the Creodrop login page using the same method you signed up with (Google, GitHub, or email and password).

If you signed up with a social provider and want to also log in with a password, you can set one from your account settings after logging in.
